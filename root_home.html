Welcome To Root Setup

What is Root?

Root is an assymmetric warfare and area control board game taking place in the woodlands with up to 4 players, featuring what seems to be adorably hand-drawn woodland creatures, but in truth they are imbued with violent tendencies. There are 10 unique factions to choose from, and each player is in control of a different class, or faction on the board. These factions all vary in playstyle and strategies, so choose the one that bets fits you. There are plenty of expansions that add a such a wide variety of new features, rulesets, gamemodes, and so much more. Root's gameplay is as captivating as its artwork. It offers a delicate balance of strategy, negotiation, and adaptability. The constantly evolving dynamics on the board make every playthrough a unique experience. From forming alliances to launching daring raids, each decision you make will have consequences.

About This Website

This website is a fan-made tribute to the game, dedicated to providing information to help you navigate the whimsical yet strategic world of Root. It provides features such as a draft system, a score taly, system, information on each faction, tips & tricks, and more. A website for all levels of players."
